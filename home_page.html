Why did we create this Website?



Welcome to the 2025 Calendar of Buddhism! We created this calendar to map together the significant Buddhist holidays across all forms of the religion. This website was produced to unify Buddhist followers from all traditions to celebrate the diverse festivities througout the calendar year. Check out the calendar's curated collection of dates accompanied by intricate descriptions to understand the day's importance. This will help viewers understand the variety of practices that contribute to the Buddhist tradtion. Whether you are a devout Buddhist or just simply interested in the culture, we hope this calendar serves to be informational and insightful about the upcoming holidays in 2025.
